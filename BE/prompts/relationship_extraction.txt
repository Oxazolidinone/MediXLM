Medical Knowledge Relationship Extraction

Source Knowledge: {source_name} (ID: {source_id})
Target Knowledge: {target_name} (ID: {target_id})
Relationship Type: {relationship_type}

Instructions:
1. Identify the medical relationship between source and target
2. Validate the relationship type is appropriate:
   - TREATS: treatment → disease/symptom
   - CAUSES: disease/condition → symptom
   - DIAGNOSES: test/procedure → disease
   - PREVENTS: preventive measure → disease
   - INDICATES: symptom → disease
   - CONTRAINDICATES: treatment ↔ condition
   - INTERACTS_WITH: drug ↔ drug

3. Extract relationship properties:
   - Strength of association
   - Clinical evidence level
   - Frequency/prevalence
   - Special conditions

4. Verify medical validity:
   - Evidence-based support
   - Clinical guidelines compliance
   - No contradictory relationships

Output:
- Validated relationship
- Confidence score
- Supporting evidence
- Additional metadata
